Mitsubishi Fuso FG4X4 Expedition Truck for Sale

Your next adventure! This 2018 Mitsubishi Fuso FG4X4 was bought new in 2019, and has an ultralight insulated fibreglass/foam camper body by Total Composites. The articulating subframe was engineered and installed by Intercontinental Truck Body.

The camper interior was built by the owner and is about 90% complete. Interior wall partitions are fibreglass honeycomb panels and the CNC cut cabinetry is 18mm Joubert Poply ultralight plywood. Galley cabinet fronts are 18mm black walnut veneer plywood. Head cabinet fronts are 18mm teak marine plywood.

The camper is weathertight and extremely well insulated and you can go camping right away, but there is still some work to be done to make a true world traveler. ROAD Overland is a local fabricator who has worked on this truck and other Fusos and can produce bar work, additional storage boxes, extended fuel tank, bumper, or anything else you desire.

Asking $125,000 USD as-is. Incomplete items are noted in bold and are negotiable.
